About us

So ... we present to you our team! Putilina Daria and Medvedev Maxim. We are 4th year students at the Institute of Computer Systems. We hope that we are very funny and resourceful guys. Do you want to know why we made this game or how could such an idea come to our mind? Everything is very simple. We are students, and we are restless. Sitting at lectures and listening to theory, and even writing a synopsis - well, it is very difficult. Of course at the institute we are taught a lot and everything is interesting. BUT! What to do if you want to learn something by yourself? That's right - go to Google and look for online courses on programming. But believe me, everything is much duller there than you think. And in our experience, having started 9 courses you can finish only 4, and successfully finish no more than 1.

Therefore, we thought, what if learning would be fun and exciting? What if we do not sit at our desks and try to learn the theory of a tooth.

This is how we came to the conclusion that you can do something similar to an online course, but only with a game in which you can run, jump, shoot, win, compete and many other things.

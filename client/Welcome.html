Game subject

Game subject

Do you want to learn how to program in Javascript? But you do not want to sit at textbooks, listen to lectures or go to the teacher? Then this game is created just for you: you can simultaneously play and learn programming.

Thanks to this game you will find yourself in the wonderful world of Trolls and even turn yourself into it. But before you is a very difficult path: first you need to get a Troll passport, then you need to choose a portal through which you can get into the world of people. You ask why the Troll in the world of people?

As we all know, people have a very strange habit - to buy a bunch of unnecessary junk and not even use it. But for us, for the Trolls, this is not junk. Trolls eat crystals, ancient books and other interesting things.

Your task is to choose a house, get there, and quietly without waking anyone to find food. But not everything is so simple. In order to show home you need to call the portal - solve the problem of programming!

But do not worry, not everything is as difficult as it seems. The Trolls Association will help and prompt you first. And by the end of the game you will already become a real professional.

How to play

How to play

The player has two game modes:

- Teaching in a programming language, ie complete course of programming from the basics.

- A competition mode where a student can improve his programming skills by competing with other students.

In this mode, the player writes immediately the entire code of the character control, then at the end of the time for writing the code, the Arena of the competitions is launched, where both players can follow the winning code. In the training mode, the player is playing level playing tasks. The character control is not done using the usual keyboard and mouse, but by executing the code written by the player in the JavaScript language. He has the ability to write the code gradually or prescribe the actions of the character to the entire level.

About us

So ... we present to you our team! Putilina Daria and Medvedev Maxim. We are 4th year students at the Institute of Computer Systems. We hope that we are very funny and resourceful guys. Do you want to know why we made this game or how could such an idea come to our mind? Everything is very simple. We are students, and we are restless. Sitting at lectures and listening to theory, and even writing a synopsis - well, it is very difficult. Of course at the institute we are taught a lot and everything is interesting. BUT! What to do if you want to learn something by yourself? That's right - go to Google and look for online courses on programming. But believe me, everything is much duller there than you think. And in our experience, having started 9 courses you can finish only 4, and successfully finish no more than 1.

Therefore, we thought, what if learning would be fun and exciting? What if we do not sit at our desks and try to learn the theory of a tooth.

This is how we came to the conclusion that you can do something similar to an online course, but only with a game in which you can run, jump, shoot, win, compete and many other things.
